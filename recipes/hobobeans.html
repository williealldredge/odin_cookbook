HoboBeans

Try these amazing hobo beans at your next get together

Here we take all the hearty beef and beans you can imagine, add a bit of spice to get that digestion going, mix it all together and BOOM... HoboBeans!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 medium yellow onion
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 8 slices bacon
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 cans (28oz) Bush's baked beans
  • 1 can pork and beans
  • 1/4 cup barbecue sauce
  • 2 tablespoons dijon mustard
  • half of a small jar jalepe√±os,sliced with juice

Steps

  1. add ground beef, onion, bell pepper, garlic salt, and onion powder. Cook until the hamburger is no longer pink. Drain excess liquid
  2. Add cooked bacon crumbles, brown sugar, beans, bbq sauce, and dijon mustard and peppers. Stir to incorporate all ingredients
  3. Crock pot high for one hour then set to warm, stir when needed.
  4. When done, the beans should be hot and bubbly and thickened
  5. Serve warm and enjoy!
